Who we are

Bafenyi, meaning "winners" in Setswana, is a community-driven organization dedicated to uplifting individuals regardless of their circumstances. Rooted in the belief that every person deserves dignity, purpose, and love, we are committed to reaching out to adults and children residing in marginalized areas of our community. Our mission is to provide both physical sustenance and spiritual nourishment, thereby combating poverty and hopelessness.

What we do

ATISA 'MULTIPLY' ADULT PROGRAM

Our adult program operates three days a week, offering a space where individuals who may not attend traditional sunday church services can encounter the word of God. Through inspirational messages and acts of kindness, we strive to remind participants of their inherent worth and purpose. Additionally, our feeding scheme provides meals for approximately 300 adults weekly. Moreover, we assist with individual social cases, such as aiding those displaced by disasters and facilitating identification processes.

KIDS' CLUB

Recognizing that not all children experience nurturing environments at home, our Kids' Club provides a safe haven where youngsters can experience love and encounter spirituality. Divided into three age groups, ranging from 4 years to Grade 8, our club sessions incorporate engaging activities such as Bible lessons, crafts, and games. Furthermore, our feeding scheme ensures that around 700 children receive nutritious meals each week. We also aim to extend our impact by offering training to ministries passionate about children's welfare, thereby multiplying our efforts to reach more vulnerable youth.

Our Approach

Accountability: We are committed to transparency and accountability in all our endeavors. Every donation received is handled with responsibility and honesty, ensuring that resources are utilized effectively to benefit those we serve.

Love and Compassion: Central to our ethos is the practice of loving others unconditionally, following the example set by Jesus Christ. By extending kindness and compassion to those in need, we foster an environment of care and support.

Community Building: Through initiatives like the construction of a community center in Promosa, we provide a space for networking, training, and collaboration among like-minded organizations. By joining hands with partners such as Feisty Females and Fire and Fragrance South Africa, we amplify our impact and create a stronger, more unified community.
